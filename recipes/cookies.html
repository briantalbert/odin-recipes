Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups AP flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Cream together both sugars and coconut oil
  3. Add in egg and vanilla, mix until smooth
  4. Sift flour with baking soda and salt
  5. Add flour mix to bowl in 2 parts
  6. Add chocolate chips
  7. Bake 10-12 minutes
