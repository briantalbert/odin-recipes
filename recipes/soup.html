Red Pepper Ditalini Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground mild Italian sausage
  • 2 boxes Red Pepper soup
  • Ditalini pasta
  • Spinach

Method:

  1. Brown sausage in stock pot.
  2. When sausage is browned, add red pepper soup and pasta.
  3. Bring to boil, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook until pasta is desired doneness.
  5. Remove from heat, add chopped spinach and stir.
  6. Very hot, allow to cool a few minutes before serving.
