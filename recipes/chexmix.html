My Chex Mix

Ingredients:

  • 1 box Honey Nut Chex
  • 1 box Golden Grahams
  • 1 box Life
  • Mixed nuts
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1.5 Tbsp Seasoned Salt
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 250 F
  2. Mix together all cereals. I use a large plastic tote, but a garbage bag works just as well.
  3. Melt the butter, mix with Seasoned Salt and Worcestershire Sauce
  4. Fill 9x13 baking dish with cereal mix, making sure you can still stir it without spilling!
  5. Pour the seasoned butter mix over the cereal, tossing and turning to coat.
  6. Bake for 1 hour, stirring halfway through
  7. Enjoy! It's good hot, but even better once cooled to room temperature!
